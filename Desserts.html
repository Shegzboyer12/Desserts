Delicious Bites Menu

Appetizers

    Fresh tomatoes, mozzarella, and basil drizzled with balsamic glaze.

    Asun is roasted goat meat that is cut into small bite sizes then stir-fried in a pepper mix.

    It is well-seasoned beef that is boiled and fried, a few pieces are then skewered with cubes of onions, bell pepper and carrot.

    A traditional Italian dish made with layers of pasta, sauce, and cheese. Lasagna can also include vegetables or ground meat.
